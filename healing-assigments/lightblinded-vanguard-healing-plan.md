# Lightblinded Vanguard Mythic Healing Plan

## Source Context

- Our report: https://www.warcraftlogs.com/reports/B2FRhGD3krpmnMC4
- Comparison report: https://www.warcraftlogs.com/reports/J8Y6BnPHzjMFhRNT?fight=15&type=healing
- Fight: Lightblinded Vanguard on Mythic difficulty.
- Similar comp reference was used to build the cooldown plan, then adjusted for our roster and Viserio import needs.

## Main Finding

The biggest healing problem was not simply missing throughput. The wipe gate was `Tyr's Wrath` clear speed before the `02:45` Sacred Toll / Trample pressure point.

- Our pull 35 averaged roughly `7.5s` to clear Tyr absorbs.
- The comparison kill averaged roughly `4.2s`.
- The plan therefore protects the first Senn overlap and explicitly calls for aggressive Tyr absorb clearing from `02:27` to `02:42`.

## Strategic Changes

- Do not spend `Yu'lon` on pull just for padding.
- Do not spend `Aura Mastery` around `01:07`; save it for the first Senn overlap.
- Use `Revival` mainly as a mass-dispel style button for full `Avenger's Shield` dispel sets.
- Make `02:24` through `02:45` the first serious healing assignment checkpoint.
