# Belo'ren Raid Defensive Windows

Suggested places to use non-healer raid defensives for our roster.

Available raid defensives from tanks/DPS:

- Warrior tank: `Rallying Cry`
- DPS/Aug Evokers: `Zephyr`
- Demon Hunter DPS: `Darkness`
- Death Knight DPS: `Anti-Magic Zone`

This is based on the exact-comp Belo'ren logs in this folder. The recurring danger points are `00:50-00:55`, `02:00-02:50`, and `04:40-05:10`.

## Suggested Baseline

| Time | Defensive | Source | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `00:50` | `Anti-Magic Zone` or `Zephyr` | Death Knight or Evoker DPS/Aug | First-minute `Voidlight Convergence` + `Burning Heart`. Good support for the planned `00:55 Rewind`; avoid overcommitting here if healing is stable. |
| `02:10-02:15` | `Rallying Cry` | Warrior tank | Start of the long mid-fight damage run. Logs often show heavy `Light Flames` / `Void Flames` pressure ramping here. |
| `02:36-02:41` | `Zephyr` | Evoker DPS/Aug | Stacks well with the common healer stack around `Spirit Link Totem`, `Aura Mastery`, and Priest cooldowns. |
| `02:50-02:57` | `Darkness` | Demon Hunter DPS | Follow-up coverage after the main mid-fight healer stack, especially if the raid is grouped. Several logs use Darkness or Zephyr around this timing. |
| `04:40-04:50` | `Zephyr` or `Darkness` | Evoker DPS/Aug or Demon Hunter DPS | Late heavy `Light Flames` / `Void Flames` window. |
| `04:50-05:05` | `Anti-Magic Zone` or second `Darkness` | Death Knight or Demon Hunter DPS | Last dangerous overlap before the kill in the 5-minute logs. |

## Defensive By Ability

### `Rallying Cry`

Best use:

- `02:10-02:15`

Alternate use:

- `02:33-02:38` if we want it inside the big mid-fight stack instead of before it.

Planning note: prefer `02:10-02:15` if healer cooldowns are already assigned at `02:36-02:41`; prefer `02:33-02:38` if the mid-fight stack needs everything layered together.

### `Zephyr`

Best uses:

- `02:36-02:41`
- `04:40-05:05`

Optional early use:

- `00:50`, if the first-minute `Voidlight Convergence` feels dangerous.

Planning note: with two non-healer Evokers, a clean setup is one `Zephyr` for the mid-fight stack and one `Zephyr` for the late push.

### `Darkness`

Best uses:

- `02:50-02:57`
- `04:50-05:07`

Planning note: Darkness is strongest if the raid is already grouped or can deliberately collapse. With two Demon Hunters, use one after the main mid-fight healer stack and one during the late burn.

### `Anti-Magic Zone`

Best uses:

- `00:50-00:55`
- `02:39-02:43`
- `04:50-04:55`

Planning note: `Anti-Magic Zone` appears in logs around all three of those timings. Since we only have one DK DPS, the cleanest default is `04:50-04:55` unless the first-minute hit or mid-fight stack needs extra coverage.
