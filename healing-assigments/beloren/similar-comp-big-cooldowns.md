# Belo'ren Similar Comp Big Cooldown Usage

Raw major-cooldown cast extraction from exact-match Mythic Belo'ren logs.

Tracked setup:

- Preservation Evoker
- Holy Priest
- Holy Paladin
- Restoration Shaman

Use this as evidence for assignment planning, not as the final plan. Repeated rotational entries such as frequent `Divine Toll`, plus single-target saves such as `Lay on Hands`, `Blessing of Sacrifice`, `Time Dilation`, and `Rescue`, should be reviewed before becoming assigned raid cooldowns.

Preservation Evoker note: five of the six exact-comp logs show `Dream Flight` casts, while report 02 shows `Stasis (Store)` / `Stasis (Release)` usage instead. This suggests Dream Flight is the common setup in these examples, with Stasis as a real alternative.

Rewind note: the common first Rewind timing is around `00:54-00:55` in reports 02, 03, 04, and 05. Report 01 uses an early `00:06` Rewind and should be treated as an alternate opener/outlier, not the baseline assignment timing.
